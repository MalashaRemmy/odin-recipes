Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water according to package instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
  4. Serve the beef mixture in the tortillas, top with cheese, tomatoes, onions, and cilantro.
